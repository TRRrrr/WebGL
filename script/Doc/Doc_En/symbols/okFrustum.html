Class okFrustum

Frustum class.

Class Summary
Constructor Attributes Constructor Name and Description
 
okFrustum()
This is commonly used in computer graphics to describe the three-dimensional region which is visible on the screen, the 'viewing frustum', which is formed by a clipped pyramid; in particular, frustum culling is a method of hidden surface determination.

Parameters:

The index of the point. 0:Intersection of near, bottom, left planes; 1:Intersection of near, bottom, right planes; 2:Intersection of near, top, right planes; 3:Intersection of near, top, left planes; 4:Intersection of far, bottom, left planes; 5:Intersection of far, bottom, right planes; 6:Intersection of far, top, right planes; 7:Intersection of far, top, left planes;
Returns:
{okVec3} The reference of the specified point of the frustum. NOTE: the returning point can not be altered outside.
